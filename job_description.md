# Senior AI Engineer - Founding Team

Redrob AI is hiring a Senior AI Engineer to own candidate ranking, retrieval,
and matching systems for an AI-native talent intelligence platform.

The ideal candidate has 5-9 years of experience, strong Python skills, and
hands-on production experience with embeddings-based retrieval, hybrid search,
vector databases, ranking systems, and evaluation frameworks such as NDCG, MRR,
MAP, offline benchmarks, and online A/B testing.

Strong candidates should have shipped real search, recommendation, retrieval,
or ranking systems to users, understand production ML tradeoffs, and be able to
balance deep technical judgment with pragmatic product engineering.

Relevant experience includes sentence-transformers, OpenAI embeddings, BGE, E5,
FAISS, Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, Milvus, Elasticsearch, OpenSearch, model
serving, feature pipelines, inference optimization, and learning-to-rank.

The role is not a fit for candidates whose AI experience is limited to recent
framework demos, candidates from purely research-only backgrounds without
production deployment, candidates who have not written production code recently,
or candidates without meaningful NLP, information retrieval, ranking, or
recommendation-system exposure.

Behavioral and availability signals matter. Strong candidates should show
active job-market engagement, recruiter responsiveness, profile completeness,
reasonable notice period, and career stability.
